# Affiliates

Public links use `?ref=AFFILIATECODE` and may include standard UTM fields. The platform stores hashed request identifiers, landing page, first and last touch, referral and conversion timestamps. Attribution follows the participant, survey, Checkout metadata, payment and report.

Commission records are unique per payment. Matching participant and affiliate email addresses are flagged as self-referrals. Administrators can disable codes, filter results, export CSV, adjust commissions and mark approved commissions paid.
